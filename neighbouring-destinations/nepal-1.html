Nepal

A heady concoction of nature, heritage, and adventure- Nepal tour promises you a wealth of experiences and a lifetime of adventure. The majestic Mount Everest, gorgeous valleys, dense Himalayan forests, gushing rivers, and Oz-like cities craft a magical spell on their many visitors. Its rich heritage, dating back to hundreds of years, illustrates its glorious past while its vibrant festivals & ever-smiling people depict its colorful culture.

Walk through the bustling streets of Kathmandu, pay obeisance at spiritual temples & stupas, visit the UNESCO-listed Durbar Squares, and take a quick drive to the heritage suburbs of Patan & Bhaktapur. Be a part of lively festivals, trek along narrow trekking trails, and breathe in the fresh mountain air of Nepal.

Nepal Special

Panoramic Landscapes

The dazzling white snow-capped peaks, the purple-green Himalayan ridges, the verdant forests, the blooming valleys and the Himalayan foothills lush with paddy fields paint the landscape in colorful hues. The beautiful towns of Nagarkot and Pokhra offer magnificent Himalayan views.

Heritage Galore

A treasure-trove of heritage, in the form of spectacular palaces & Durbar Squares, greets you in and around the capital city of Kathmandu. Many of these brilliantly built buildings are listed as UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Pilgrimage

This country of stunning temples and impressive stupas is a major pilgrimage for both Hindus and Buddhists. The Pagoda styled temple of Lord Pashupatinath, a manifestation of Lord Shiva, and the spectacular Swayambhunath & Boudhnath stupas are the silent sentinel of Kathmandu. Lumbini, the birthplace of Buddha, and Janakpur, the birthplace of Goddess Sita, are the two most-visited pilgrimages of Nepal.

Adventure and Wildlife

Nepal with its sky-high mountains, fathomless valleys, and spectacular Terai region, is an adventure enthusiast’s Holy Grail. You may trek the Annapurna Circuit, go whitewater rafting, try bungee jumping in deep canyons, and go paragliding in Pokhra. Imagine the thrill of sky-diving through a curtain of clouds over the magnificent Mount Everest or cannoning through tumbling waterfalls- the adventure here is never-ending. You may also opt for an exciting wildlife safari in Chitwan National Park to spot the snow leopards, red pandas, and Tibetan wolves.

Alluring Destinations of Nepal

Kathmandu

Veiled behind a curtain of colorful prayer flags and protected under the watchful gaze of Buddha, Kathmandu is a delightful combination of chaos, culture, and cacophony. Dotted with UNESCO-listed heritage palaces & Durbar Squares, spectacular stupas & temples, and the living Goddess Kumari, Kathmandu is a fascinating city. Check out the lively markets, quirky cafes, and the quintessential Freak Street of its touristy district- Thamel.

Enquire Now Tour Packages

Bhaktapur

Located around 8kms from the Kathmandu city, Bhaktapur takes you back in time to the era of the Malla rulers of Nepal. A UNESCO-listed Heritage site, Bhaktapur is a fine specimen of Newari architecture and culture. Pagoda-styled temples, the magnificent Golden Gate, and the Palace of 55 windows are some of the highlights of Bhaktapur. Its many lanes are dotted with weavers, sculptors, potters, and other artisans showcasing the artistic heritage of the city.

Patan

Just 5kms away from Kathmandu, Patan, also known as Lalitpur, holds a beautiful blend of Buddhism and Hinduism within its folds. The architectural brilliance of the Durbar Square, the artistic talents of its Thangka painters, and its stunning temples & monasteries depict the timeless legacy of Patan.

Nagarkot

Perched on the rim of the Kathmandu Valley, the little town of Nagarkot offers you majestic views of the Himalayas. Watch a colorful sunrise or sunset, walk down the trekking trails, and soak in rustic Nepal experience.

Pokhara

A breathtaking panorama of stunning mountains and sparkling lakes, Pokhara is the adventure-land of Nepal. Take a boat ride on Phewa Lake, go paragliding & whitewater rafting, explore Davis Falls, or trek up to the Annapurna Base Camp- it is fun unlimited!
