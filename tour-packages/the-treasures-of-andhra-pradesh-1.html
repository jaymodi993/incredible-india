The Treasures of Andhra Pradesh

Duration: 07 Nights / 08 Days Destinations: Hyderabad - Nagarjunkonda - Warangal - Vijayawada - Amravati – Visakhapatnam
Tour Highlights
Hyderabad Visiting Quli Qutub Shahi Tombs, Golconda Fort with sound and light show.
Warangal Thousand Pillar Temple, the Warangal Fort, the Ramappa Temple and Ghanpur Ruins.
Vijayawada Visakha Temple, Rishikonda Beach and Indira Gandhi Zoological Park.

Day 01: Arrive Hyderabad

Arrive Hyderabad Airport, Our representative will assist and transfer you to your hotel. Afternoon proceed on a sight seeing tour of the City Visiting Quli Qutub Shahi Tombs, Golconda Fort with sound and light show Necklace Road and drive around hussain sagar lake. Overnight stay in Hyderabad

Get Free Tour Quotes

Day 02: In Hyderabad

Next morning we take you to explore the entire city. Visit Charminar and walk through the exquisite palaces embellished with intricate carvings. Go inside the Lad Bazar, located just outside the Charminar and shop for some beautiful pearl jewellery which make Hyderabad famous in the world. Also visit the magnificent Birla Mandir, Mecca Masjid, and Salarjung Museum housing some priceless antiques and art. Spend the evening at leisure. Overnight at hotel. Overnight stay in Hyderabad

Day 03: Hyderabad (Excursion of Nagarjuna Sagar)

Morning we will proceed to Nagarjuna sagar visiting the famous Nagarjuna Sagar Dam. You will also get to see the Nagarjuna Konda and Museum after lunch we move on to the Ethipothal Waterfalls, Anupu and return to Hyderabad and spend the night in the hotel.

Day 04: Hyderabad – Warangal (150 Kms. - 3.5 Hrs.)

Morning we will leave for Warangal, which is just a few 150 kms. from Hyderabad. We will visit the Thousand Pillar Temple, the Warangal Fort, the Ramappa Temple and Ghanpur Ruins. Overnight stay at Warangal.

Get Free Tour Quotes

Day 05: Warangal - Vijayawada (270 Kms. - 6 hrs.)

After breakfast drive to Vijayawada and check into the hotel. Visit the famous Kanaka Durga Temple, Akana and Madana Caves and Malleswaraswami Temple. By afternoon, you will leave for an excursion to Amravati and visit ruins of Buddhist Stupas and Temples. By late evening, you will return to the Vijayawada hotel and stay overnight.

Day 06: Vijayawada  - Visakhapatnam  (350 Kms. - 7.5 hrs.)

After breakfast we drive to Visakhapatnam and check into the hotel and rest for a while. Following soon after will be a short trip to the Ramakrishna Beach. You will come back to the hotel by evening and stay overnight.

Day 07: In Visakhapatnam

Next morning we take you to explore the numerous tourist attractions in Visakhapatnam. Visit the places like Visakha Temple, Rishikonda Beach and Indira Gandhi Zoological Park. Thereafter, you will proceed towards Arraku Valley and Borra caves, situated near the city. By late evening, you will return to the hotel and stay overnight.

Day 08: Visakhapatnam Departure

In the morning transfer you to airport to board the flight for onward destination.
